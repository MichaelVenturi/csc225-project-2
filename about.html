MF DOOM

MF DOOM is a British rapper, songwriter and record producer. Real name Daniel Dumile, he is most notable for his "super villain" stage persona. His iconic metal mask is reminiscent to the Marvel Comics super-villain Doctor Doom. He has gone by several stage names in his career, including Metal Fingers and Viktor Vaughn (another reference to Doctor Doom, whose real name is Victor Von Doom).

He has a very unique style of rapping, and has been apart of many collaborative projects including Madvillain (with Madlib), Danger Doom (with Danger Mouse), and Czarface Meets Metal Face (with Czarface). DOOM has been active in the rap game for over two decades, and is widely considered as the king of the underground and one of the best rappers of all time.
